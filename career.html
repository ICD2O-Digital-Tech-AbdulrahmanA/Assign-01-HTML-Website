Early Career

Luka Dončić began his professional career with Real Madrid in the EuroLeague.

Real Madrid

Luka made his debut in the EuroLeague at just 16 years old and won the MVP award in 2018.

NBA Career

  • Drafted by the Atlanta Hawks in 2018, traded to the Mavericks.
  • Won NBA Rookie of the Year in 2019.
  • Multiple-time NBA All-Star.
  • Led Mavericks to playoffs multiple times.
  • Recently drafted in 2025 to the Los Angeles Lakers.
